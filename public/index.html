SETTINGS

Input mode
Push-to-talk: hold a key/button, speak, release. Open mic: KAEL listens continuously and sends automatically when you pause — tap the orb to pause/resume, or to interrupt KAEL mid-reply. Open mic needs a quiet-ish room (it goes deaf while KAEL is speaking to avoid hearing itself).
Push-to-talk key
Space
Hold this key — or a mouse button — anywhere on the page to talk; release to send. You can also press & hold the orb. Click Rebind, then press the key or mouse button you want (Esc cancels).
Voice
★ marks higher-quality natural / neural voices. For the smoothest, most Airy-like options, open KAEL in Microsoft Edge — it exposes the full set of natural voices.
Premium voice
Far smoother and more natural than any browser voice (OpenAI tts-1-hd). Uses your OpenAI key — about a dollar a month at normal use. Leave off to keep the free browser voice above. shimmer is the softest, most airy option.
Brain
Temperature default
Switch the local model (it must be pulled via ollama pull) and how varied replies are — lower temperature is more focused, higher is more creative. "default" uses the model's own setting.
Persona
KAEL's character and instructions (its system prompt). Edit to retune its tone or role; Reset restores the built-in default.
Memory
What KAEL durably remembers about you. Remove a single fact with ✕, or wipe it all. (Separate from the chat — "new chat" keeps these.)
Awareness local · off
Off — KAEL isn't watching your screen.
OCR (off): reads exact on-screen text — only helps on screens dense with small text, and adds a couple seconds per glance. Collect training data (off): the one place KAEL saves screenshots — it stores each glance as an image+caption pair in data/training/ (local, gitignored) to build a fine-tuning dataset for later. Your ✎ corrections re-label them. See scripts/finetune/. KAEL glances at your screen and webcam with a local vision model and remembers what you're doing, so it has real context about your day. Nothing is recorded — frames go only to the model on your PC, no images are ever saved, and nothing leaves the machine. You choose what screen to share; the webcam light blinks on each glance. Pause anytime here or from the browser's “stop sharing” bar. (Sensitive-screen filtering is OFF — KAEL describes whatever is on screen.)
Proactive — talks on its own off
KAEL speaks up on its own — greetings, observations, check-ins, encouragement — based on what it sees you doing, not only when you talk to it. It picks up your focus automatically from what you tell it during the day (just say or type “I'm gonna work on the kael project” and it sets the focus itself) — or type one in the box above. With a focus, it also keeps you on track (nudges drift, suggests breaks, offers help when you're stuck). Needs awareness on. The brain reasons over your activity timeline (and, for auto-focus, your message) — the small local model is too weak to judge this, so it defaults to your gpt-oss cloud model, meaning that text goes to it for the decision. Pick a local model for full privacy (but it'll rarely speak).
Tasks
KAEL captures tasks automatically when you mention them in chat (“I need to finish X by Friday”), prioritizes them by urgency & deadline, and can break any task into steps. Tick to complete a task or a step; ask KAEL “what should I work on?” anytime and it answers from this list.
